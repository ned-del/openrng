## Guidelines for sending

1. **Don't mass-send.** Pick groups where this genuinely adds value.
2. **Personalize the first line** — reference something specific about their group.
3. **One message only.** No follow-ups unless they respond.
4. **If they're not interested, move on.** No convincing, no pushing.
5. **Time it right** — send during active hours for their timezone.
