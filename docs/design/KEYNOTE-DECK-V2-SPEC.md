## Design Direction

Not a startup deck. An **Apple keynote**.

### Visual Rules
- 🌑 Full-bleed black backgrounds
- ✨ One powerful sentence per slide
- 🎰 Beautiful product renders instead of bullet points
- 🎬 Motion-first storytelling (slides designed for animation)
- 💎 Minimal text (20–40 words per slide)
- 🟢 Premium cyan/emerald accent colors
- 🎯 Large typography with lots of whitespace

### Narrative Arc

```
Can you trust luck?
    (One glowing SPIN button)

What if you could watch trust being created?
    (Cinematic verification timeline)

Trust isn't a claim. It's an experience.

OpenRNG
The infrastructure for verifiable digital experiences.
```

### Tone
- This should feel like **the unveiling of a new category**
- Not a pitch for funding — a declaration of what's coming
- No apologizing, no "we're early" — pure confidence in the vision

## 3-Minute Product Keynote Video

### Concept

Not a pitch. A **masterpiece**.

Almost no words. Just:
- Beautiful music
- Beautiful animations
- Spin
- Verify
- Smile
- Share

### Ending

```
OpenRNG

Watch Trust Being Created.
```

### Production Standard
- Apple-quality motion design
- Cinematic pacing (every second intentional)
- No voiceover narration (music + visuals + minimal text)
- Sound design: satisfying, premium, ASMR-adjacent
- Target: the single artifact that explains OpenRNG to everyone — gaming CEOs, regulators, developers — in 3 minutes

### Why This Matters
> "If we can produce that video at Apple-quality, it will become more valuable than a 100-page whitepaper."

### Production Path
1. play.openrng.io must be built first (the video captures the real experience)
2. Screen capture the actual product in use
3. Add motion graphics for the verification timeline
4. Professional music licensing or composition
5. Final cut: 2:30–3:00, not a second longer
