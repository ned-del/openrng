## Value Propositions (3 pillars)

### Verifiable
Every entropy object carries its source provenance, confidence score, cryptographic signature, and optional blockchain anchor. Verify any VEO without trusting the provider.

### Quantified
The Entropy Confidence Score (ECS) measures entropy quality across six dimensions — freshness, diversity, independence, manipulation resistance, verification success, and availability. Request entropy at the trust level your application requires.

### Anchored
For the highest trust level, entropy is anchored to blockchain smart contracts. Anyone can read the contract and confirm the entropy existed at a specific block height and time.
