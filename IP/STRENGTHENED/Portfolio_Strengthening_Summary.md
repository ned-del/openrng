- Non-amplification enforcement as independent claim (not just feature)
- Trust chain audit traversal as independent claim with specific computational steps

### Patent #5 — Continuous Trust Health
**Previous Score:** 8.0 → **Projected Score: 9.5**

- Explicit distinctions from SIEM, APM, GRC, Cyberscope, and Yaslioglu
